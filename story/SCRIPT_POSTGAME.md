## POST-GAME OVERVIEW

After the credits, the player wakes up in their house in NULLTOWN. The world has changed — NPC dialog across the region reflects the aftermath of the Bureau's exposure. The RIFT SHOALS are now fully accessible. Gym Leaders can be rematched at full power. QUIN is available for a final rivalry battle.
